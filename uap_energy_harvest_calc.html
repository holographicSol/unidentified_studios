ℹ️ System Information

How it works: This UAP harvests electromagnetic energy from environmental fields using advanced collection arrays. In Earth's atmosphere, it taps the natural electric field gradient (100-150 V/m). In space, it harnesses solar wind plasma and magnetospheric fields (hundreds to thousands of volts). The collected energy powers the vehicle's thrusters and propulsion system.

High Voltage, High Current Design: The system generates high voltage (kilovolts) with strategically scaled current from large collection arrays. Power = Volts × Amps, so even modest current across high voltage arrays produces usable power. Conversion circuits rectify oscillating fields into DC power, stored in capacitor banks and supercapacitor arrays.

Optimal Environments: Plasma clouds near gas giants and solar wind regions provide the strongest fields. Earth's upper atmosphere and ionosphere also work well. The system scales with array size—larger collectors gather more current from the same field.
